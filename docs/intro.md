# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This textbook covers the fundamental concepts, practical implementations, and cutting-edge research in embodied intelligence.

## About This Textbook

This textbook is designed for:
- AI Students
- Robotics Students
- Engineers
- Hackathon Participants
- Panaversity Learners

## What You'll Learn

This comprehensive guide covers:

- **Part I: Foundations** - Introduction to physical AI, history of humanoid robots, embodied intelligence theory, and sensor systems
- **Part II: ROS 2 and Simulation** - Understanding ROS 2, simulation environments (Gazebo, Unity, NVIDIA Isaac), and robot description formats
- **Part III: Advanced Topics** - Vision-language-action systems, voice interaction, locomotion, manipulation, and cognitive reasoning
- **Part IV: Implementation and Deployment** - Capstone project, hardware requirements, and deployment strategies
